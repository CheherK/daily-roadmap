Weekly — Sunday evening (non-negotiable)
W1
Write your ONE mission for the coming week One specific, completable deliverable. Not a task list. Not "work on the app." One thing you will finish or ship.

The 6 laws — always

I
Shame does not produce change. Guilt does. "I did something bad" is actionable. "I am bad" is paralysis. Always translate to behavior.
II
Never miss twice. Miss once — it's an accident. Miss twice — it's a pattern. The only rule is: always show up the next day.
III
Environment beats willpower every time. Structure the environment so good behavior is easy and temptation requires effort. Willpower is fuel — spend it wisely.
IV
2 hours of real work is better than 8 hours of distracted work. Protect the 2-hour block like it is the most valuable appointment of your day — because it is.
V
Procrastination is an emotional problem, not a time problem. When you avoid — ask what feeling you are running from. Name it. Then take the smallest possible action anyway.
VI
The gap is 3–5 years of real effort, not a lifetime. You already spent 5 years earning a degree. You know how to sustain effort. The conditions just need to be right again.

The version of you that graduated as a software engineer from Tunisia — disciplined, capable, persistent — is the same person reading this. He is not gone. He just needs the right conditions to come back. You are building those conditions, one day at a time.
